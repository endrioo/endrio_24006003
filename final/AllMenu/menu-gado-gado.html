Gado-Gado

Gado-Gado is a traditional Indonesian salad made from lightly boiled or steamed vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and hard-boiled eggs. It is served with a generous amount of rich, creamy peanut sauce and garnished with crackers and fried shallots. This healthy and satisfying dish offers a perfect balance of flavor and texture.

Ingredients:
Cabbage, spinach, bean sprouts, long beans, tofu, tempeh, boiled egg, cucumber, peanut sauce, fried shallots, crackers

1 portion (approx. 350g)
