Rendang Daging

Rendang is a legendary Indonesian dish from the Minangkabau ethnic group of West Sumatra. Made by slow-cooking beef in coconut milk and a fragrant mix of lemongrass, garlic, turmeric, ginger, and chilies, this dish is rich, tender, and full of depth. The result is a deeply caramelized, spicy meat that melts in your mouth.

Ingredients:
Beef, coconut milk, lemongrass, garlic, shallots, galangal, ginger, turmeric, red chilies
Serving Size:
1 portion (approx. 250g)
