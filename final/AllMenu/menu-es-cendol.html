Es Cendol

Es Cendol is a refreshing traditional Indonesian dessert drink made with green rice flour jelly (cendol), sweet coconut milk, and palm sugar syrup. Served over ice, this chilled treat is perfect for hot days. Its creamy, sweet, and slightly smoky flavor makes it a favorite across Southeast Asia.

Ingredients:
Green cendol (rice flour jelly), coconut milk, palm sugar syrup, ice cubes, pandan leaves, salt

1 glass (approx. 300ml)
