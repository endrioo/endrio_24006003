Mie Goreng

Mie Goreng is a flavorful Indonesian fried noodle dish commonly made with yellow noodles stir-fried in a savory blend of garlic, shallots, sweet soy sauce, vegetables, and eggs. Often served with chicken, shrimp, or tofu, and topped with fried shallots and sliced cucumber or tomato. It's a beloved comfort food across the country.

Ingredients:
Yellow noodles, garlic, shallots, sweet soy sauce (kecap manis), cabbage, carrots, egg, chili, chicken/shrimp/tofu
