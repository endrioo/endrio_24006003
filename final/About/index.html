Our Story

Preserving Indonesian culinary traditions since 1995

The Heart of Indonesian Cuisine

Founded in 1995 by Chef Budi Santoso and his wife Sari, Warung Nusantara began as a small family kitchen with a big dream: to share the authentic flavors of Indonesia with the world. What started as a humble warung in the bustling streets of Medan has grown into a beloved culinary destination that celebrates the rich tapestry of Indonesian gastronomy.

Our name, "Nusantara," represents the diverse archipelago of Indonesia, and our menu reflects this beautiful diversity. From the spicy rendang of West Sumatra to the sweet gudeg of Yogyakarta, every dish tells a story of our homeland's culinary heritage.

We believe that food is more than sustenance‚Äîit's a bridge that connects people to culture, tradition, and each other. Every recipe we serve has been passed down through generations, carefully preserved and lovingly prepared to maintain its authentic character.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Budi Santoso

Founder & Head Chef

With over 30 years of culinary experience, Chef Budi learned the art of Indonesian cooking from his grandmother in a small village in West Sumatra. His passion for preserving traditional recipes while adapting them for modern palates has made Warung Nusantara a cornerstone of authentic Indonesian dining.

"Cooking is not just about combining ingredients," says Chef Budi. "It's about understanding the soul of each spice, respecting the traditions of our ancestors, and creating moments of joy for every person who sits at our table."
